Career

Popularly known as the "Father of the Digital World," Steve Jobs was an American entrepreneur, investor, co-founder of Apple Inc. Not satisfied with a single achievement, he moved on to make history in the world of consumer electronics...

Childhood and Early Life

Born on February 24, 1955, Steve Paul Jobs was the adopted son of Paul Reinhold and Clara Jobs. His biological parents were Abdulfattah "John" Jandali and Joanne Carole Schieble...

Career

  • In 1976, Jobs, with Wozniak, founded "Apple Computer Company."
  • In 1985, difference of opinion between Jobs and CEO John Sculley led to Jobs resigning from Apple...
  • In 2001, the company entered the music world with iPod and iTunes...
  • In 2007, Jobs revolutionized the cellular phone business with the launch of the iPhone...
